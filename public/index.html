About

This game consist on chances. You will have the opportunity to buy and sell pre-selected houses across the country. The goal its to have the highest ending balance based on your decisions. The trick is that you will not have monetary information of the houses until the final balance. Be wise and have fun.

Instructions

Select play. Once started, please select one of the pictures on the screen. There will be three groups of selections until the final screen. Once finished you will see the final results of your selections.

Hints to play





Educate

Information it's important in the buisness. Make sure to have and understand the most possible information about the transaction.

Appraisal

Appraisals are helpful tools to have knowledge of the the property but not always have the final information.

Location

Location its very important to have a clue about the value of the property. Take an eye of this when playing.
